FAQs

Discover answers to the most common questions about ICAS services, rights, and support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Immigration & Visa

What should I do if my SEVIS record is terminated?
Contact your school's DSO immediately. You may be eligible for reinstatement or need to explore re-entry or change of status.
What is the difference between CPT and OPT?
CPT is for employment during studies. OPT is post-graduation work authorization, often with STEM extension.
Can I stay in the U.S. after my OPT ends?
You must leave the country, transfer to another school, or change visa status within 60 days.
What if I travel while on OPT?
You should carry your passport, F-1 visa, EAD card, and job offer letter. Traveling without employment may risk re-entry.

Legal Rights & Support

What are my rights if stopped by the police?
You have the right to remain silent and request an attorney. You are not required to answer immigration status questions.
What if I'm arrested?
Do not resist. Ask for a lawyer and do not sign anything without legal counsel. Contact your consulate if you're a non-citizen.
Can I get legal help if I can't afford it?
Yes, legal aid services and public defenders are available for low-income individuals.
How do I expunge a dismissed case in Georgia?
Apply for record restriction with the arresting agency. ICAS can help with guidance and resources.

Student Support

What happens if I drop below full-time enrollment?
Your SEVIS may be terminated unless the DSO approves a reduced course load for medical or academic reasons.
Can I change schools while on an F-1 visa?
Yes. Your SEVIS record must be transferred and you need a new I-20 from the new school.
What should I do if I'm dismissed by my school?
Speak with your DSO immediately. You may qualify for reinstatement or a transfer.
Where can I find emotional support?
ICAS offers mental health support, peer groups, and access to professional counselors.

Women’s Support

I'm experiencing domestic violence. What should I do?
Call 911 if in immediate danger. ICAS can connect you to legal and shelter services confidentially.
Can I work if I'm on a dependent visa?
Some dependent visas like H-4 are eligible for work with EAD authorization. Check with USCIS.
Is support available for women with children?
Yes, ICAS helps with childcare, school support, and family services.
Will my situation be kept confidential?
Absolutely. ICAS maintains strict confidentiality for all assistance inquiries.

Small Business & Employment

Can I start a business on a student visa?
Generally no. Consult with an immigration attorney about exceptions like passive investment.
What businesses can I start as a green card holder?
You may own and operate any legal business in the United States.
What permits do I need for a retail business?
You'll likely need a business license, tax ID, and local permits like health or signage permits.
Does ICAS offer business mentorship?
Yes. We host workshops and connect aspiring entrepreneurs with experienced mentors.

General Community

How can ICAS help me?
ICAS provides support in immigration, legal aid, women’s safety, business, and student empowerment.
Is ICAS a law firm?
No, but we refer you to trusted legal partners and advocates in our network.
